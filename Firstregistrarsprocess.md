# 📘 FIRST REGISTRARS – FULL OPERATIONS MANUAL (A–Z)

## 🧭 1. OVERVIEW OF OPERATIONS

### 1.1 What First Registrars Does

First Registrars is a share registry and investor services company responsible for:

- Maintaining shareholder records
- Processing dividends
- Managing share transfers
- Handling probate (deceased shareholders)
- Supporting capital market transactions
- Regulatory compliance with SEC Nigeria / NSE (NGX)

## 🏗️ 2. CORE SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

### 2.1 Key Systems

- E-Stock System (Core Registry Engine)
- KYC & Verification System (1stCheck / CleCheck)
- Dividend Processing Engine
- Probate Ease System
- Customer Portal (Online Access)
- CRM / Call Center System
- Document Management System (DMS)

## 👥 3. SHAREHOLDER ONBOARDING PROCESS

### 3.1 New Shareholder Creation

Step-by-Step:

Receive investor data from:

- IPO / Public Offer
- Secondary market purchase

Capture:

- Full Name
- Address
- Bank Details
- CSCS Account

Validate using:

- BVN
- NIN
- ID Upload

Output:

- Unique Shareholder ID
- Entry in E-Stock database

## 🔄 4. SHARE TRANSFER PROCESS

### 4.1 Types

### 4.2 Workflow

- Share certificate

- Update register

## 💰 5. DIVIDEND PROCESSING (CRITICAL)

### 5.1 Dividend Lifecycle

#### A. Declaration

- Company declares dividend
- Board approval obtained

#### B. Qualification

- Closure of register
- Snapshot of eligible shareholders

#### C. Processing

- Calculate dividend per shareholder
- Apply withholding tax (10%)
- Generate payment schedule

#### D. Payment

- Direct credit via bank
- E-dividend mandate required

#### E. Reporting

- Payment reports
- Reconciliation logs
